## Send-Cadence Recommendation (10 lines)

1. **Day 0 (launch day):** Send Template 1 to the bottom 20 ranked brands — highest urgency, ride the leaderboard news cycle.
2. **Day 0–1:** Send Template 2 to marketing/SEO leads at brands ranked 21–60 — warm them with competitive framing while the leaderboard is fresh.
3. **Day 1–3:** Send Template 3 to 30–50 hand-picked brands not on the leaderboard, prioritising those with active SEO/content budgets.
4. **Day 3 (follow-up 1):** One-line bump on Template 1 threads with no reply: *"Did you get a chance to check your breakdown?"*
5. **Day 5 (follow-up 2):** For Template 2 no-replies, send a 2-sentence stat: include one data point about AI search share of voice growth to re-engage.
6. **Day 7 (follow-up 3 / last touch):** Breakup email — *"Closing this out — happy to send your free snapshot if the timing ever works."*
7. **Cap sequences at 3 touches total** to protect sender reputation; do not exceed 50 new sends/day per sending domain.
8. **A/B test subjects** on first 20 sends per template before scaling — track open rate; if below 25%, swap subject line.
9. **Prioritise LinkedIn + email combo** for Template 1 targets — view their LinkedIn profile the day before sending to trigger a profile-view notification that warms the cold open.
10. **Pause all outbound if reply-to-open ratio drops below 5%** — investigate deliverability before continuing.
