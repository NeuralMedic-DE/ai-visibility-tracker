## Recommended Fixes

1. **Address Zapier vs n8n pricing narrative:** CM-02 negative sentiment across GPT-4o and Perplexity signals active negative framing around Zapier pricing relative to n8n. Publish transparent pricing comparison content that acknowledges n8n's free tier but highlights Zapier's ease of use, reliability, and total cost of ownership for non-technical teams.

2. **Reposition for budget-conscious no-code users:** UC-01 and AL-02 both suffer from Zapier being perceived as expensive. Create content targeting "affordable no-code automation" and "free tier automation" use cases, surfacing Zapier's free plan and starter pricing in content that LLMs can cite for cost-framed queries.

3. **Improve n8n alternatives framing:** The AL-02 gap reveals LLMs don't think of Zapier when users ask about n8n alternatives. This is a missed opportunity given Zapier's 6,000+ app integrations. Publish comparison content explicitly positioned as "Zapier vs n8n: which is right for your team" targeting non-technical and SMB audiences.
